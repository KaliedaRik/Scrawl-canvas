Test purpose

  • Check that all fonts load as expected.
  • Check the HandleX selections perform as expected, particularly for right-to-left fonts.
  • Check the HandleY selections work as expected for various fonts.
  • Check scale/roll/flip settings work as expected on the entity - the text should remain in its bounding box at all times.
  • Check the letter and word spacing attributes work as expected, and that the text remains within its bounding box at all times.

To note: Safari-based browsers do not (yet) support letter and word spacing settings within the <canvas> element.
