Note that, in general, Scrawl-canvas avoids scaling the canvas when it stamps entitys onto it, preferring instead to pre-calculate scaling effects on entity dimensions etc. Smoothing is most likely to be an issue when scaling copied data from an asset, as may happen with Picture entitys and Pattern styles.
